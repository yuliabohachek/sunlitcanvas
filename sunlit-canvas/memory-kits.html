How it works

01
You send everything
Share your folder — all the photos, unedited, in any order. Answer five short questions about what this memory means to you.
02
I find the ten frames
One image for each type of moment — a surprise, a face, a detail, a transition, a quiet second. Your memory reduced to what it actually is.
03
I make the object
A hand-stitched zine, a keepsake card, a kraft envelope. Your full gallery goes into a private archive linked by QR inside the zine.
04
It arrives in 7 days
Delivered anywhere in Europe. The reveal happens when you open the envelope — not in a preview, not before. That is part of the object.

Before I begin

Five questions.
No brief needed.

You don't need to know which photos you want. That's my job. I only need to understand what this memory feels like — so that the ten frames I choose feel true, not just well-composed.

  • 1What would you most want to remember about this, ten years from now?
  • 2Was there a moment that surprised you?
  • 3Is there something important that isn't in any photo?
  • 4One word or feeling that describes the whole thing.
  • 5Who is this kit for — you, or someone else?
